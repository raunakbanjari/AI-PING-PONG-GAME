AI PING PONG GAME










INSTRUCTIONS

  • MOVE YOUR MOUSE UP AND DOWN IN ORDER FOR YOU PADDLE TO MOVE
  • IF YOU HIT THE BALL WITH YOUR PADDLE, YOU GET 1 POINT
  • IF YOU MISS, THE COMPUTER GETS A POINT
  • IF THE BALL HITS ANY PADDLE, THE SPEED OF THE BALL INCREASES
  • IF THE COMPUTER GETS A POINT, THE SPEED OF THE BALL RESETS
